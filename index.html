About

I am a master's student in Transportation at Tongji University and received my bachelor's degree in Transportation from Chang'an University. My work combines transportation engineering with human factors, statistical modeling, virtual-reality experimentation, physiological sensing, and machine learning.

I am particularly interested in trustworthy intelligent-driving systems: how automated vehicles communicate with road users, how driver and pedestrian states can be measured, and how experimental evidence can be translated into safer products and evaluation methods.

01 / ABOUT

I work across transportation engineering, human factors, and AI.

My core interest is trustworthy intelligent mobility: how automated vehicles communicate with pedestrians, how physiological and behavioral signals reflect human risk states, and how experimental evidence can be translated into safer systems and evaluation methods.

I combine controlled VR experiments, questionnaire and physiological data, mixed-effects modeling, traffic-data analysis, computer vision, and AI-assisted document workflows.
